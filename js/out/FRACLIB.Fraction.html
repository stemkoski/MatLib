Class: Fraction

FRACLIB.Fraction(numerator, denominator)

A Fraction object represents a rational number. It has a numerator and (nonzero) denominator. Supported operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, negation, inversion. Fractions are always reduced to lowest terms.
